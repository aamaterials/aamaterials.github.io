Post Docs and Research Assistants

Dr Xu Chen Postdoctoral Researcher

His current project mainly focuses on the development of nanocarriers based on MOFs to encapsulate small drugs and macromolecules such as siRNA or mRNA for cancer therapy.

Dr Sergio Mercado Postdoctoral Researcher

Sergio is a postdoc researcher within the group. His research focuses on the use of metal organic frameworks as drug delivery systems.

Lik Hong Wee Postdoctoral Researcher

Lik is a scientist with a distinguished career in the field of nanoporous materials. His current project focuses on the shaping of MOFs for hydrogen storage.

Dr Shiyuan Zhang Postdoctoral Researcher

I play with the magic of X-ray crystallography to discover the wonderful chemistry and material world in molecular level, such as crystal engineering and molecular chirality.

Dr Elena Avila Postdoctoral Researcher

Elena obtained her PhD in 2019 in Seville focusing on the use of FLP analogy on organometallic compounds to evaluate the cooperative metal-ligand effect. Her current research focuses on the use of MOF for the development of hybrid, light-harvesting materials to boost chemical reactions.

Harrison Partner Lab technician

My main responsibilities include managing the groups cell-culture requirements, along side research into MOF based drug delivery systems.
