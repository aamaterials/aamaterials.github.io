Post Docs and Research Assistants

Dr Salame Haddad Postdoctoral Researcher

Salame (Sam) obtained his PhD in this group. His research is focused on the use of MOFs for targeted intracellular delivery of bioactive molecules. Prior to starting his PhD, Sam obtained his BEng in Chemical Engineering from the American University of Beirut, before pursuing the MPhil in Advanced Chemical Engineering course at CEB. In his spare time Sam enjoys travelling, music, playing the piano, cooking, and sports.

Dr Xu Chen Postdoctoral Researcher

His current project mainly focuses on the development of nanocarriers based on MOFs to encapsulate small drugs and macromolecules such as siRNA or mRNA for cancer therapy.

Dr Rocio Bueno Perez Research Associate

Rocio is a postdoc researcher in the group. Her research focuses on the computational high-throughput screening of nanoporous materials to perform adsorption based separations.

Dr Shiyuan Zhang Postdoctoral Researcher

I play with the magic of X-ray crystallography to discover the wonderful chemistry and material world in molecular level, such as crystal engineering and molecular chirality.

Dr Robin Lab Technician

Robin Babu completed his PhD from South Korea, mainly focused on the structural investigation of MOFs that enhance the CO2 fixation reactions. His current work is mainly focused on the scaleup synthesis of Monolithic Metal Organic Framewroks in collaboration with the Immaterials Lab.
